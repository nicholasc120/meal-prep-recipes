# Garlic Butter Steak Bites:

https://www.tiktok.com/@panaceapalm/video/7293182411188260129

## Macros
5 Servings - 451 Cal, 41gP
5 Servings without sauce - 425 Cal, 41gP

## Ingredients
- 2lbs top sirloin (1420 Cal, 182gP)
- Tsp salt & pepper
- cooking oil spray
- 15g unsalted butter (105 Cal)
- 2 cloves garlic (finely chopped)
- Tsp dried parsley (or fresh!)

Crispy Potatoes:
- 900g potatoes (diced into cubes) (600 Cal, 18gP)
- tsp paprika
- tsp onion powder
- tsp garlic powder
- tsp dried parsley
- tsp salt & pepper
- cooking oil spray

Peppercorn Sauce: 
- 5g unsalted butter (35 Cal)
- 1 cup (260ml) beef stock (brand: Tesco)  (10 Cal)
- 10g crushed and whole peppercorns (from a peppermill)
- 2 cloves garlic (finely chopped)
- 45g light cream cheese (brand: Philadelphia lightest) (65 Cal, 3gP)
- 5g cornstarch mixed with 1-2 tsp of water (prevents clumping) (20 Cal)

Air fryer and Oven Settings 🔥
- Air fryer: 200°C | 15-20 Minutes (shake halfway)
- Oven: 220°C | 20-25 Minutes (shake tray halfway)

## Instructions
### Potatoes
- Dice potatoes into cubes and season with smoked paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, parsley, and salt & pepper.
- Give a quick spray of cooking oil and mix until fully coated
- Air fry or oven for specified amount
### Steak
- Cook on high heat until browned
- add 15g unsalted butter, 2 cloves minced garlic, and parsley and stir to melt
### Peppercorn sauce
- Mix unsalted butter, beef stock, and crushed peppercorns to a simmer.
- Add chopped garlic, cream cheese, turn heat down to melt
- add cornstarch + water to thicken

Storage & Heating ♨️
- Store in meal prep boxes with the sauce separated (I used plastic shot glasses). Refrigerate for up to 5 days.
- When it’s time to eat, reheat the potatoes for 2-3 minutes in the air-fryer till crispy. Microwave the steak bites for 2-3 minutes till hot. Microwave the sauce separately for 15-20 seconds, then pour over your steak bites and enjoy!
