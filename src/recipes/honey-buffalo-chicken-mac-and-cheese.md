# Honey Buffalo Chicken Mac & Cheese

https://www.tiktok.com/@aussiefitness/video/7263052285742288130?lang=en

## Macros - 4 portions
512 Cal, 79gP

## Ingredients
- 700g Diced Chicken Breast (raw weight - seasoned with 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1tsp garlic powder & 1 tsp onion powder)** (750g/3 breasts - 900 Cal, 168gP)**
- 350g Low Fat Cottage Cheese
- 65g Low Fat Cream Cheese (Philadelphia - or 4 light laughing cow cheese wedges)
- 35g Natural Cheddar (Dairyworks)
- 80ml Low Fat Milk
- 20ml Hot Sauce (Franks Redhot Buffalo Sauce)
- 320g Macaroni or Elbow Pasta (dry weight - Lulupasta Protein Elbow Pasta)
- 1 Tbsp Honey & 1 Tbsp Buffalo Sauce (mix with cooked chicken)

## Instructions
### Chicken
- Season chicken breast with salt, smoked paprika, garlic powder & onion powder. Mix and set aside
- Cook chicken on medium heat for 8 to 10 minutes
- Turn off heat and add 1 tbsp buffalo sauce and 1 tbsp honey
