# Honey BBQ Chicken Mac and Cheese

https://www.tiktok.com/@aussiefitness/video/7405914982753357064?lang=en

## Macros: Per Serving - 5 Total
With 3 chicken breasts:
(with Normal BBQ Sauce: **619 Cal**)
(with sugar free BBQ Sauce: **594 Cal**)
**45g protein**

## Ingredients:
- 700g Boneless & Skinless Chicken Breast (raw weight) *(840 Cal, 157g protein)* (3 chicken breasts - 750g, 900 Cal, 168g protein), (2 chicken breasts - 500g, 600 Cal, 112g protein)
- 1 Tsp Salt, 1 Tsp Onion Powder, 1 Tsp Smoked Paprika & 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
- 1 Tsp Olive Oil *(40 cal)*
- 25g Honey *(76 cal)*
- 85g Sugar Free BBQ Sauce (Hughes Sugar Free BBQ Sauce - or reduced sugar BBQ sauce) *(151.78 Cal normal) (28 Cal sugar free)*
- 320g Macaroni (dry weight - San Remo Elbow Pasta) *(1129 Cal)*
- 340ml Fat Free Evaporated Milk (Carnation Light & Creamy - can be substituted for low fat / fat free milk) *(236 Cal, 24g protein)*
- 80g Light Cream Cheese (Philadelphia) *(160 Cal, 8g protein)*
- 1.5 Tsp Chicken Stock Powder (Vegeta)
- 100g Grated Light Cheddar Cheese (Dairyworks Natural Cheddar Cheese) *(402 Cal normal cheese, 25g protein)*

## Instructions
- Cube chicken and season with 1 Tsp Salt, 1 Tsp Onion Powder, 1 Tsp Smoked Paprika 1 Tsp Garlic Powder, 1 Tsp Olive Oil and mix
- Cook chicken on medium heat 4 mins each side
- Once cooked, remove from heat and add honey and BBQ sauce and mix
- Cook pasta
- While pasta is cooking, heat a pan on medium heat and add Fat Free Evaporated Milk, Light Cream Cheese, Chicken Stock Powder, and grated cheddar cheese
- Once the sauce is smooth and creamy, mix it with the pasta
