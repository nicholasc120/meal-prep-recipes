# Hamburger Helper Meal Prep 🍔 🧀

https://www.facebook.com/share/r/14o8weiW1M/

## Macros (per serving/bowl) ⬇️
- 541 calories
- 42g protein
- 48g carb
- 11g fat

Servings: 5 bowls
Prep time: 5 minutes
Cook time: 25 minutes

## Ingredients (5 servings) ⬇️
- 1 tsp olive oil (40 cal)
- 1.5 lb lean ground beef (1088 cal, 136 gp)
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- 3 cups beef broth (51 cal, 8gp)
- 1 cup fat free milk (122 cal, 8gp)
- 2 tbsp half and half (39 cal)
- 3/4 cup marinara (120 cal, 6gp - rao's marinara sauce)
- 300g macaroni (1058 cal, 38gp)
- 56g cheddar cheese (230 cal, 13gp)

## Instructions
- cook beef over medium heat with 1 tsp olive oil
- once beef is partially browned, add seasonings and garlic and mix
- mix in beef broth, fat free milk, marinara until boiling
- once boiling, add uncooked macaroni. cover and lower heat to cook for 13 - 15 minutes
- Once done, mix in cheddar cheese
