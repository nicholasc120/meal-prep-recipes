# Beef bulgogi

https://www.maangchi.com/recipe/easy-bulgogi
cook this on a nonstick pan

##  5 portions - 580 Cal, 40gP
- 2 pound beef tenderloin, top sirloin, or skirt steak, slice into ⅛-inch-thick slices (1824 Cal, 184gP)
- 8 garlic cloves, minced
- 4 green onion, chopped
- 4 tablespoons soy sauce (34 Cal)
- 3 tablespoons sugar (150 Cal)
- 6 tablespoons water
- 2 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
- 2 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds, coarsely ground
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

- 250g Uncooked Rice (895 cal, 16gP)
