# Garlic Parmesan Chicken Alfredo

https://www.instagram.com/p/CpfpI7RDY9a/

## Macros
Per serving (makes 5):
505 calories
44g Protein
52g Carbs
15g Fat

## Ingredients:

- Garlic Parmesan Chicken marinade:
- 10g olive oil (2 tspns)
- 2 tblspn white wine vinegar
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
- 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon pepper
- 10g Honey (2 tspns)
- 20g finely grated Parmigiano Reggiano

- 16oz boneless skinless chicken thighs

Stealth Health Alfredo Sauce:
- 350g 2% cottage cheese
- 80g 1% milk
- 85g fresh Parmigiano Reggiano
- Pinch of salt, pepper and garlic powder
- 320g linguine (dry weight)


## Steps
- Assemble marinade and marinate chicken while prepping everything else
- Add Alfredo Sauce to blender for 1 to 2 minutes until completely smooth
- Air fry chicken at 375 for 12 minutes
- Cook pasta
