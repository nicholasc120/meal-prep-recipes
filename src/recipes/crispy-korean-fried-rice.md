# Crispy Korean Fried Chicken

https://www.tiktok.com/@aussiefitness/video/7339489761884704008?lang=en

## Four servings:
571 Calories* | 47gP
\* Assuming all 45g Cornstach is used

## Ingredients:
- 750g Diced Chicken Breast (3 chicken breasts) (900 cal, 168 gP)
- 1 Tsp Salt, 1 Tsp Garlic Powder, 1/2 Tsp Black Pepper
- 1 Tsp Olive Oil (40 cal)
- 45g Cornflour/Cornstarch (not all of this gets used) (~180 cal)
- 1 Tbsp Olive Oil (119 cal)
- 1/3 Cup Salt Reduced Soy Sauce (Kikkoman Salt Reduced Soy Sauce) (50 cal, 7gP)
- 1/3 Cup Water
- 2 tbsp/6 cloves Minced Garlic 
- 1 tbsp  (about an inch)Minced Ginger
- 1 Tbsp Gochujang (56 cal)
- 40g Honey (122 cal)
- 3 tsp Rice Wine Vinegar (20 cal)
- 2 tsp Sesame Oil (81 cal)
- 3g Cornflour Mixed With Water (~1.25 tsp cornstarch + 1.25 tsp water) (11 cal)
- 200g Uncooked Rice (716 cal, 13gP)

## Garnishings
- Black & White Sesame Seeds
- Spring Onion/Scallions
- Sliced Red Chilli

## Instructions
- Dice chicken breast and add salt, garlic powder, black pepper, and olive oil and mix
- Coat chicken in corn starch and mix
- add 1 tbsp olive oil on HIGH heat*
- Add chicken and let cook through on high heat until golden brown and crispy all over
- Set aside and in a pan, add soy sauce, water, minced garlic and minced ginger, gochujang, honey, rice wine vinegar, sesame oil, and cornflour mixed with water.
- Turn heat to medium and stir gently until sauce thickens. Add chicken and mix well to combine

## IMPORTANT NOTES:
\* When cooking the chicken make sure the oil is piping hot before placing the chicken in the pan. If the oil is cold it will soak into the cornflour.
You can also air fry / oven bake the chicken instead.
Slowly add the cornflour mixed with water while stirring until you have the right consistency for the sauce.
