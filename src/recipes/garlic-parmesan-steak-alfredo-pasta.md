# Garlic Parmesan Steak Alfredo Pasta
https://www.tiktok.com/@jalalsamfit/video/7428301090057211169?lang=en

## Macros Per Serving (6 Servings Total)
503 Calories | 41g Protein

## Ingredients (To Make 6 Servings)

### Garlic Parm Steak Bites
- 2lbs top sirloin (1420 Cal, 182gP)
- 1.5 Tsp Salt
- 1.5 Tsp Black Pepper
- 2 Tsp Italian Herbs
- 1.5 Tsp Parsley
- 1.5 Tsp Garlic Powder
- 1.5 Tsp Smoked Paprika
- 2 Tsp Olive Oil
- 30-40g Light Butter (for cooking + extra once cooked) (210 Cal)
- 2 Tsp Garlic Paste / Minced Garlic
- 20g Grated Parmigiano Reggiano (87 Cal, 8gP)

### Alfredo Pasta
- 80g White Onion Chopped (32 Cal)
- 1 Tbsp Chopped Garlic
- Extra Seasoning (1 Tsp each of Salt, Pepper, Italian Herbs, Parsley, Smoked Paprika)
- 1 cup milk (100 Cal, 9gP)
- 130g Light Cream Cheese (260 Cal, 7gP)
- 40g Grated Parmigiano Reggiano (174 Cal, 16gP)
- 210g Uncooked Pasta (740 Cal, 26gp)
- 50-60ml Pasta Water (if needed if sauce is too thick)

### Instructions
- Cook steak bites in light butter 2 mins each side on medium high heat
- lower heat add butter, fresh garlic, grated parmesan. mix and set aside
- to a pan, add onion and chopped garlic. cook for 5 minutes before adding extra seasoning and mix
- add milk, cream cheese, grated parmesan, and pasta water.
- stir till smooth and let bubble before adding cooked pasta

Important Cooking Notes

- Marinate the steak bites for 30mins before cooking, will have better flavour
- Add the milk and cream cheese on low heat then mix till smooth. Let it bubble slightly till thick then add the cooked pasta. Mix till well combined
- This meal prep freezes especially well and is easy to reheat. Place in a pan or microwave for 5 mins on low with a dash of milk in the pasta
