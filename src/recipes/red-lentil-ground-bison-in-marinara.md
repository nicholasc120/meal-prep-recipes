# Red Lentil Ground Bison Pasta

## Ingredients
- 425g Red Lentil Pasta (1450 Cal, 110gP)
- 454g Extra Lean Ground Bison (817 Cal, 90gP)
- 3-4 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 diced brown onion
- 1 Jar of Marinara sauce (Rao's 770ml - 616 Cal, 12gP)

## Steps
- Cook garlic in pan until fragrant, then add garlic. add ground bison and cook 
- Add Marinara sauce to pan and mix
- Cook Red Lentil Pasta separately
- Measure and combine into 5 portions
