# Pulled Pork

https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/92462/slow-cooker-texas-pulled-pork/

## Macros: 
- 8 servings (no buns): 380 Cal, 48gP
- 8 servings (with buns): 510 Cal, 52gP

## Ingredients
- 1 teaspoon vegetable oil (40 Cal)
- 1 (4 pound) pork tenderloin (2168 Cal, 380gP)
- 1 cup barbeque sauce (480 Cal)
- ½ cup apple cider vinegar (25 Cal)
- ½ cup chicken broth (7 Cal)
- ¼ cup light brown sugar (207 Cal)
- 1 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard (9 Cal)
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce (13 Cal)
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 1 extra large onion, chopped
- 2 large cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 ½ teaspoons dried thyme

- 8 hamburger buns, split (dempsters - 130 Cal, 4gP)
- 2 tablespoons butter, or as needed

## Notes
if you freeze it with the buns, you need to defrost it a day in advance. After defrosting, I warm the buns in the air fryer and then put the defrosted coleslaw inbetween the buns and the pulled pork but i think i forgot to write down what ingredients are in the coleslaw
